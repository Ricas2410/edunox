Most Popular Questions

The questions we get asked most often

Is University of the People really free?

Yes, UoPeople is tuition-free. Our fees cover support services, not education costs.

What documents do I need?

You'll need your ID and academic results (WASSCE, BECE, or equivalent).

How long does the application process take?

Typically 1-3 weeks depending on the university and completeness of documents.

Do you help with scholarships?

Yes, we assist with finding and applying for scholarships to reduce costs.
