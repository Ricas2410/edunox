What We Do vs. What Universities Do

EduBridge Ghana (Us)

  • • Application guidance and support
  • • Document preparation assistance
  • • Scholarship application help
  • • Digital literacy training
  • • Travel to remote communities

Universities (Them)

  • • Provide the actual education
  • • Set admission requirements
  • • Make admission decisions
  • • Award degrees and certificates
  • • Charge their own fees (if any)
